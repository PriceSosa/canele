From Hwy 5 take Glendale Blvd exit. From State Route 2 take San Fernando Road or Fletcher Drive. Click on map above for details.

Parking

We do not offer valet, but there is public parking available on Glendale Blvd and neighboring streets, with meters active 8:00 - 8:00.

Our Neighborhood

In the six years since we've been here, Atwater Village has become a destination for good eats, drinks, shopping, exercise, and entertainment. Details magazine recently declared the village one of The Six Hottest Emerging Neighborhoods In the World's Greatest Cities. We encourage you to stroll and enjoy the community we're so proud to be a part of.
